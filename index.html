About the Forum

The Monipur High School and College Cultural Forum (MSCCF) is the premier organization dedicated to nurturing the artistic and cultural potential of our students. We bridge the gap between academic excellence and creative expression.

Our Facilities & Wings

Music & Vocals

Professional training in classical, folk, and modern songs with expert guidance.

Dance & Choreography

Dedicated studio space for practicing traditional and contemporary dance forms.

Drama & Theatre

A platform for stage acting, scriptwriting, and performing arts production.

Debate & Literature

Weekly workshops on public speaking, logic, and creative writing contests.

Art & Multimedia

A wing focused on digital media, photography, and fine arts exhibitions.

Auditorium Access

Exclusive access to school auditorium facilities for rehearsals and programs.
